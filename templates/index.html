Plants Information

Rice

Rice is a staple food for more than half of the world's population. It is grown in flooded fields called paddies. The rice plant has long, narrow leaves and is usually grown in tropical and subtropical climates. Rice requires a warm environment and is highly sensitive to temperature changes, pests, and diseases. It plays a crucial role in global agriculture and food security.

Wheat

Wheat is one of the oldest and most important crops cultivated for its grain. It is a major food source worldwide, known for its nutritional content, especially carbohydrates and proteins. Wheat is grown in temperate climates and requires a significant amount of water. It has various varieties, including spring and winter wheat, each with different growing seasons.

Maize

Maize, also known as corn, is a highly versatile crop grown primarily for its grain. It is a crucial food source for both humans and animals. Maize is grown in a wide range of climates, from tropical to temperate zones. The plant has tall stalks with large, broad leaves and is known for its high yields. It is used in a variety of products, from food items like cornmeal to biofuels and industrial materials.
